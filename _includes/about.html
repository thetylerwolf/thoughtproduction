What's in a name?

We are Thought. Our name comes from the thought that we put into every detail of our work. Great design requires attention to every pixel, graphic and interaction. That is what we do and that is what we are.



Thought Philosophy

Data is the lifeblood of 21st century business. Valuable insights pulse over the wire with every interaction and process iteration. In order to tap its full potential, data needs to be understood. We use all available information and context to inform our data-driven design process, including user feedback. Thought works to breathe life into your data to find its meaning and surface its valuable insights.
